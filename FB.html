POST UTME AND DE ADMISSION SCREENING EXERCISE FOR THE 2023/2024 ACADEMIC SESSION

 

This isto inform all candidates who scored a minimum of 140 points in the 2023 UTME and D.E candidates with Credit/Merit/Pass in HND,ND,NCE,IJMB or its equivalent in relevant areas from a recognised tertiary institution, that the screening of candidates for admissions into various Undergraduate programmes has been scheduled as follows:

1. Online Application and Registration for the PostUTME/DE screening will commence from Monday,14th August to    Monday,11th September,2023;and

2.Physical screening of candidates at various Faculties/Departments will be as follows:

ELIGIBILITY AND REQUIREMENTS:

 

Candidates who possessed the above-mentioned requirements are eligible to present themselves with the following documents at the venue for physical screening:

 

1. Original and photocopies of their credentials,

 

2. Original and photocopy of UTME result slip/DE JAMB slip,

 

3. Evidence of payment for Post UTME/DE screening (Non-refundable fee of ₦2,000 only),

 

4. Acknowledgement Slip and a copy of Online Screening Form

 

PROCEDURE FOR PAYMENT AND REGISTRATION:

 

1. Candidates are to upload their O’level result into JAMB profile/portal,

 

2. Visit www.ksusta.net and click on "BUY POST UTME RRR CODE" to select the screening type (UTME or D.E),

 

3. Enter your JAMB Registration Number and other details to Generate the RRR Code invoice,

 

4. Proceed to any Bank Nation-wide for payment of ₦2,000 only, return to www.ksusta.net and click on "APPLY ONLINE" to enter your purchased RRR Code to create Screening Account,

 

5. Click on "SUBMIT" after completing the form,

 

6. Print your Acknowledgement Slip and a copy of the Online Screening Form.

 

 NOTE:

         1. All candidates are required to upload their O’level results with JAMB;

 

2. Successful candidates will have to check their Jamb Profile to accept admission being offered to them into KSUSTA;

 

and

 

3. All admitted candidates have a grace of four (4) weeks within which to accept approved admission with JAMB. Failure to which such admission will be deleted and results to ineligibility to be admitted again.
